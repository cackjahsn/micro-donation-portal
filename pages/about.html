About CommunityGive

Our Mission

CommunityGive is a micro-donation platform designed to make charitable giving accessible, transparent, and impactful for local communities. We believe that even the smallest contributions (starting from RM1) can create significant positive change when combined.

How It Works

1. Browse Campaigns

Find causes you care about

2. Scan & Donate

Use QR code for instant payment

3. Track Impact

See how your donation makes a difference

Our Values

  • Transparency: Every donation is tracked and reported
  • Accessibility: Designed for everyone, regardless of technical skills
  • Security: Bank-level security for all transactions
  • Community Focus: Built for local impact
  • Low Cost: Minimal fees to maximize impact
